## What you get

- **Complete fills indexing** with real-time ingestion and backfilled history.
- **Low latency** access through regional endpoints (EU/JP) designed for production.
- **Simple, stable REST schemas** for “minute-one” integration.
- A roadmap toward **real-time WebSocket streams** and a unified **app/dashboard**.

## Who it’s for

- Quant and strategy builders who need **fast, reliable fills**.
- Data engineers building **pipelines and analytics** on top of indexed history.
- App developers who need **predictable REST** endpoints with consistent payloads.
