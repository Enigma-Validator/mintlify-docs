## What you get

- **Complete data indexing** — fills, analytics, completed trades, users, funding, liquidations, vaults, TWAPs, HIP-3, EVM, and builder data.
- **247M+ historical trades** indexed and [freely available](https://trade-export.hypedexer.com/) for the community.
- **Low-latency access** through a global load balancer routing to the nearest region (EU/JP).
- **Production-ready REST & WebSocket APIs** with stable schemas for immediate integration.
- **5,000 free API credits** on every new account — no approval required.
