## Single endpoint, global routing

All API requests go through a single endpoint:

```
https://api.hypedexer.com
```

A **global load balancer** automatically routes your requests to the nearest infrastructure:

| Your location       | Routed to          |
| ------------------- | ------------------ |
| Europe & nearby     | Frankfurt (EU)     |
| Asia-Pacific & nearby | Tokyo (JP)       |

You don't need to choose a region — the load balancer handles it transparently based on your geographic location. Both regions expose the same paths, parameters, and data.
