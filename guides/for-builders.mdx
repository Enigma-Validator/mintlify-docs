## Free infrastructure for early-stage builders

We believe in supporting the Hyperliquid ecosystem from day one. HypeDexer provides **free access to our full infrastructure** for early-stage teams and public goods projects.

**What this includes:**

- Full access to all REST and WebSocket endpoints
- Custom endpoint development tailored to your specific requirements
- Direct engineering support from our team
- Dedicated infrastructure when your workload requires it

**How it works:**

We offer this free of charge until your project has its first paying customers and a sustainable revenue stream. Several teams already rely on HypeDexer as their primary data layer — we are committed to growing alongside you.

## Custom endpoints for your stack

Unlike generic API providers, we actively develop **custom endpoints on demand** to match your exact data requirements. If a standard endpoint does not cover your use case, we will build it — at no cost for qualifying builders.

Examples of custom work we have delivered:

- Aggregated builder fee breakdowns by coin and timeframe
- User-level ledger summaries combining transfers, deposits, and withdrawals
- Real-time HIP-3 oracle price statistics at 1-minute granularity
- Dedicated market-maker feeds with isolated infrastructure
